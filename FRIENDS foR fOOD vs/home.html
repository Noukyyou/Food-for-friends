Friends for food

After spending some time in Sri Lanka I can say that I have come to love that country, because of the Corona Pandemic I am back in NL.

In May 2021 I read that the President had introduced a ban on fertilizer imports in Sri Lanka. I informed friends that this could sometimes lead to major problems in the country, because it is precisely they who rely on Agriculture.

In April 2022 I could no longer hear how many people had fallen from 3 to 2 and then to 1 meal a day. I decided to raise money from family and friends to support SL in their food needs.

This is how the "Friends for Food" project was born.
