Our achievements

The first field



We started our endavour on a piece of land of a local that had a patch of unused land. We have unweeded the land and then prepped it for local greens.

The green house



We noticed that we needed a green house for certain crops to survive. With the donation we were able to build a greenhouse on may 2022.

The Classroom



To educate the local families we needed a classroom. A safe space where the locals can learn about how to treat the crops in a organic way.

The second field



To feed more families we needed more land so we made contact with a local that had unused land.
